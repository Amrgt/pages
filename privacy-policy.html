Privacy Policy

YouTube Summarizer — Chrome Extension · Last updated: February 27, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or sell your personal data. The extension processes YouTube video content solely to generate summaries for you.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how YouTube Summarizer ("the Extension", "we", "our") handles information when you use our Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

The Extension accesses the following data only when you actively use it:

  • YouTube video metadata: Video title, URL, and transcript/captions of the video you are currently viewing, used solely to generate a summary.
  • No personal data: We do not collect your name, email address, browsing history, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No tracking: We do not use analytics, cookies, or any tracking technologies.

3. How We Use Information

Video content accessed by the Extension is used exclusively to generate text summaries for your personal use. Data is processed in real-time and is not stored on any server after the summary is delivered.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension may use third-party AI APIs (such as OpenAI or similar services) to generate summaries. When this occurs:

  • Only the video transcript or caption data is sent to the API.
  • No personal or identifying information is transmitted.
  • Third-party services are subject to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Storage & Security

We do not maintain any databases or servers that store user data. Extension preferences (if any) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs and are never transmitted externally.

6. Permissions

The Extension requests only the minimum Chrome permissions necessary to function:

  • activeTab: To access the current YouTube page content when you activate the extension.
  • storage: To save your local preferences (e.g., summary length, language).

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
